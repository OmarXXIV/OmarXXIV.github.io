MiTracker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how MiTracker collects, uses, and protects your information.

Location Data

MiTracker uses your location data to automatically track your trips and calculate mileage. Location data is stored locally on your device and is only used for the purpose of tracking your trips.

  • Location tracking only occurs when you enable Automatic Drive Detection
  • You can disable location tracking at any time in Settings
  • Location data is never sold to third parties

Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information to provide our services:

  • Vehicle information (for trip tracking)
  • Trip data and mileage records

All personal information is stored locally on your device and is never shared without your explicit consent.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device.

  • Local data is protected by your device's security features
  • You can export or delete your data at any time from within the app

Advertising

MiTracker displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • General location information
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's privacy settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

MiTracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements
  • Apple MapKit - For map and location services

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Children's Privacy

MiTracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
